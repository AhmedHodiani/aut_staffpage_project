About

The Deanship of Scientific Research was established along with the establishment of the university at the beginning of the academic year of 2015/2016 to undertake the implementation of the policy of scientific research and the organization and support of its affairs according to the scientific research standards.

The Deanship of Scientific Research undertakes one of the most important functions of the university which is knowledge industry and employing and disseminating cutting-edge technological skills to create the appropriate environment for scientific research in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan through offering the facilities needed to support scientific research project , publishing in international refereed journal that have a high Impact factor, and participating in local, regional, and international conferences, seminars, workshops. The Deanship gives incentive awards for authentic, innovative, and scientific research by distinguishing researchers. The deanship is responsible for stimulating the appropriate environment to promote scientific production of faculty members and students, and the deanship contributes to pushing the scientific process of the university forward and its development and support.

Our Vision

Excellence, global pioneership, creativity, and innovation in the creation of productive and research-oriented environment for community service issues and in line with contemporary technological developments.

Our Mission

Increasing the support of scientific research and the dissemination of knowledge, creativity and innovation in all areas by using available technological means in accordance with the perspective of overall high quality to meet the local and regional needs, and all that is based on the transformation of scientific and technical production pattern from being an individual creativity into mass production.

Values

  • Ethics of Scientific Research
  • Originality and creativity
  • pioneership and innovation
  • Professionalism and transparency
  • Cooperation and shared responsibility
  • High-quality local community service

Objectives

  • Preparation of the Strategic Plan for Scientific Research at the university.
  • Raising the level of outstanding research work and activating its role in the development of society.
  • Achieving leadership and excellence in scientific research and achieving creativity and innovation in all fields.
  • Providing information bank of scientific research at the university.
  • Cooperation with the Jordanian, Arab and international universities in various fields of research.
  • Consolidating the ethics of scientific research and the dissemination of scientific culture among researchers.
  • Stimulating researchers at the university and encouraging them to work within multi-disciplinary research teams.
  • Supporting scientific research and production and supporting the applications for participation in scientific conferences, seminars and workshops locally, regionally and globally.
  • Activating the role of the Deanship of Scientific Research by making the Deanship get the membership in the global research networks.
  • Documenting the production of scientific research at Aqaba University of Technology

Tasks

The dean of the scientific research undertakes the following tasks:

  • Preparing the annual budget for the Deanship of Scientific Research
  • Implementation of the University's policy of scientific research in terms of organization, support and publication.
  • Seeking funding sources for scientific research from within and outside the university.
  • Collecting information on scientific research projects conducted at the university, and following them up.
  • Contacting centers and institutions related to scientific research within and outside the Kingdom and coordinating with them.
  • Providing periodic reports on scientific research conducted at the university.
  • Any other matters related to scientific research.

The Achievements of the Deanship

  • Preparing the instructions and pillars of scientific research projects.
  • Preparing the instructions of attending refereed scientific conferences.
  • Preparing the instructions of publication in specialized refereed scientific journals.
  • Preparing the instructions of giving rewards for faculty members in exchange for publishing research in international journals with high Impact Factor.
  • Development of scientific research models to be compatible with the requirements of quality assurance in the university in all areas related to scientific research.
  • Building a database of the funded scientific research projects and the researches published in specialized refereed scientific journals and conferences.
  • Increasing the means of cooperation and communication with all the sides interested in scientific research within Jordan, such as universities, the Higher Council for Science and Technology, and Scientific Research Support Fund, and Support to Research, Technological Development and Innovation in Jordan (SRTD II).
  • Increasing the means of cooperation with regional and international parties interested in scientific research, such as " European Union, the Arab network for scientific research, and the Federation of Arab universities, and The Federation of the Universities of the Islamic World ".

The Board of Scientific Research

The Council supervises the scientific research and scientific publication, and it is headed by the Dean of Scientific Research. The Council includes representatives of the various faculties at the university and representatives of the local community with competence and experience, The Scientific Research Council undertakes the following tasks:

  • Proposing the general policy for scientific research priorities at the university and their means of implementation.
  • Developing the plan of scientific research project and the pillars of cooperation and coordination with other relevant scientific research institutions.
  • Preparing the necessary instructions for organizing the affairs of scientific research, including its promotion, coordination, support, follow-up, evaluation, and publishing.
  • Developing and implementing the necessary conditions and terms for keeping the university's rights and researchers' rights of the scientific research whether singly conducted at the university or in cooperation with others from outside the university
  • Discussing the budget of the scientific research and recommending its inclusion in the university's budget.
  • Recommendation to fund the publication of valuable research and publishing translated, evaluated, and written manuscripts.
  • Examination and approval of scientific research projects.
  • Endorsing the annual report of the Deanship of Scientific Research.
  • Considering any other matters related to scientific research

Translation and Publication

The Deanship of Scientific Research supervises the preparation of regulations and instructions of funding the scientific research published in the university. The Deanship receives researches, books, and translated studies and then evaluates and prepares them for publication within a certain mechanism to ensure the rights of the university on the one hand and the rights of authors on the other hand.
