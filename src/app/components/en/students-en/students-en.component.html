Library

The university library is located on the first floor of the university's main educational building. It is a large library extending over 1200 square meters, and it is adorned with glass facades overlooking the interior garden and, thus, allowing natural light to enter the library. The library can accommodate about 350 students at one time.

The Library collection includes about 29,000 pieces of books, periodicals, references, and encyclopedias, and work is underway on the expansion of the library books to support the newly proposed disciplines at the university. There are many references available in the library at the present time revolving around natural and human sciences, information technology, financial and administrative sciences, engineering, and pharmacy.

Sports

Sports is at the heart of Aqaba University's attention. Aqaba University of Technology provides the following sports facilities:

  • A gym area extending over 1000 square meters where ground tennis, volleyball, basketball, and handball can be played. This gym includes fitting rooms, bathrooms, and showers.
  • Outdoor football court with a stadium.
  • Six outdoor basketball courts.
  • Three outdoor handball courts
  • Three outdoor ground tennis ball courts.

Student's Accommodation

Because Aqaba University of Technology is interested in attracting students from outside the city of Aqaba, it has established on- campus accommodation for male and female students. The university dormitory can accommodate 328 students, and it has spacious rooms overlooking the sea. The female dorm can accommodate 264 female students, providing them with spacious rooms in a safe area inside the university.

  • Each room is a master bedroom (with a private bathroom and Shower).
  • Each room has two beds , a wardrobe , offices for study, two chairs , a small refrigerator , a sink provided with cold and hot water.
  • There are shared kitchens, dining rooms, reading rooms, and TV rooms.
  • The university has a main restaurant offering three meals a day, a store, a cafeteria and a laundry.
  • The monthly financial cost per a double room is JD 100 per a student.
  • The single-bed rooms are rented on demand with the cost of JD 160.

Transportations

The University provides buses to transfer students from the city of Aqaba to the university and vice versa. The buses' station is located near the courtyard that is beside Zaatari Hotel and Aqaba Buses' Station Complex at King Talal Street. The university buses can take students to and from the University at specific times announced every semester and these times can be changed from one semester to another depending on traffic density. The bus journey between Aqaba the university takes about 20 minutes.

The subscription fees are per one academic semester. For the students who have been admitted into the university since (2016-2017),the first and second semester bus fees are JD 100 only, and the summer course bus feesare JD 50 for the students residing at Aqaba. As for the students residing at the university accommodation, the subscription fees of the summer semester are JD25 during the regular semesters and JD 12.5 during the summer semester.
