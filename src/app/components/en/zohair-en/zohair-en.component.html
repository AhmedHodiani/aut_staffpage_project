Zuhair Al-Sebaei Program

Based on the directives of His Majesty King Abdullah II bin Al Hussein, may God preserve and protect him, to support sciences and education to raise the efficiency and level of Jordanian youth to meet the requirements the labor market. Aqaba University of Technology is pleased to offer grants and loans to support outstanding and incapable students in participation with public and private sectors to support this kind of students to give them the opportunity to pursue their university education.

Who is Zuhair Al-Sebaei?

“Dr. Zohair Ahmed Sebai has nearly four decades of active community medical service. He graduated from Ain Shams Faculty of Medicine, Cairo, in 1962, studied tropical medicine at the Bernard Nocht Institute, West Germany and obtained his master and doctor degrees in public health from Johns Hopkins School of Hygiene and Public Health, USA, in 1969. The author carried out the field work for his doctorate degree in public health in a nomadic community in Saudi Arabia, studying the health of the Bedouins and their family medical care program. During his work in the Ministry of Health (1969-1973) as a director of planning and in King Saud University (1974-1982) as a professor of community medicine and founding dean of the Faculty of Medicine at Abha, he has been occupied with various aspects of public health. This has been expressed in his research and teaching in health planning, health manpower development, and medical education. His main objective is to interrelate medical education with community health problems and needs” (Retrieved from www.enritsch.com)

Program’s Idea

Providing scholarships for Jordanian students who want to attend studying in AUT. These scholarships cover only partial of tuition fees. For continuity, these scholarships might be converted to be a good loan system where students can pay back the money when they get job after finishing their study to be re-granted loans to other students in order to ensure the continuity of the idea and achieve its generous goals.

Beneficiaries

All needy Jordanian students who wish to study at Aqaba University of Technology and provide the fulfill the conditions for this program.

Goals of the program

  • Contributing to raise the standard living of poor students by providing them with the opportunity to obtain partial scholarships that help them complete their university education process.
  • Promoting the principle of equal opportunities among Jordanian students in all governorates through their access to equal educational opportunities, regardless of their financial capabilities.
  • Enhancing the principle of competitiveness among Jordanian students on the basis of fairness, merit, and transparency

What is Zuhair Al-Sebaei Program offer?



Scholarships Loans

The scholarships are only for students who succeed in high school or pass an intermediate diploma and wish to study at Aqaba University of Technology and within one of the disciplines listed in the table below.

Grant amount: 400 JD for one time only

Major Available Scholarships
Pharmacy 2
Architecture Engineering 4
Civil Engineering 4
Physical Therapy 2
Medical and Radiological Imaging 4
Artificial Intelligence 4

The grant amount of the scholarship is 400 JD and it is offered once for each succeed applications and it will be discounted from the tuition fees.


Scholarship rules:

  • The applicant must be of Jordanian nationality
  • To be a new student and wish to study at Aqaba University of Technology in the second semester of the academic year 2020/2021
  • To submit documents proving the family's income
  • To submit documents showing the location of residence


Scholarship Application

Loans are offered for all students who studying at AUT in all majors.


Loan Rules

  • For AUT students.
  • To submit documents proving the family's income.
  • To submit documents showing the location of residence.

The Loan amount is determined by the committee depending on the submitted applications.
